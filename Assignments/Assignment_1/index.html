Why I chose Comp-Sci

I have always been interested in electronics but more specifically, computers. Mix that with a love for continous learning and you get someone who may or may not choose to pursue a Comp-Sci degree. Obviously in my case I chose to pursue it, but it did take me awhile. I graduated the year of 2020 and it is now 2023, so roughly 2 years it took for me to decide if Computer Science was for me. I think I made the right choice!
